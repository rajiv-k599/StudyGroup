StudyGroup is a web application that enables users with similar interests to connect and collaborate with one another. The platform facilitates easy creation and joining of rooms where users can communicate and share resources such as articles and videos. The application offers group chat and notification features to keep users up-to-date with the activities in their rooms. StudyGroup promotes collaboration and knowledge-sharing, providing an interactive platform for learning and growth. Whether you're a student looking to connect with peers or a professional seeking like-minded individuals, StudyGroup is a powerful tool to help you achieve your goals.
